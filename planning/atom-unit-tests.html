01🖼️

Medium · 媒介

测: Image 2 能否从"画"读懂 · 不是"渲染". 看画布肌理 / 笔触 / 颜料堆积.

👁️ 看: 笔触是否真"刷"出来 · 还是光滑 CGI · 画布纹路有没有
02🎨

Style · 画家风格锚

测: Image 2 对真实画家风格语汇的理解度. 这是产品最核心原子.
A landscape scene with one small figure standing, in the style of Caspar David Friedrich, German romanticism, atmospheric and contemplative. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 画家标志语汇有没有命中 · 雾感 / 大尺度 / 人渺小
03🎯

Subject · Rückenfigur 背影者

测: Image 2 对艺术专有术语的理解(Rückenfigur = 德语"背影人物"流派)
A Rückenfigur — a solitary figure seen from behind, anonymous, face never visible, wearing a dark wanderer's cloak, holding a weathered walking staff, standing on a generic mountain ridge. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 人是背着还是正对 · 脸露不露 · 术语懂不懂
04🌫️

Scene · 场景氛围

测: 复合场景描述("山 + 云海 + 暮色")Image 2 能否各部分都出
One small figure on a moss-covered craggy mountain summit emerging from drifting low clouds and sea fog at late dusk, distant silhouetted peaks visible through the mist. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 雾 / 云 / 暮色 / 山峰 4 样齐不齐
05💡

Lighting · 柔光

测: Image 2 能不能"不戏剧" · 做弥散柔光 · 抑制 god rays
A landscape with one small figure, lit by entirely soft diffuse atmospheric light only, no hard rays, no dramatic spotlights, no volumetric god-rays, gentle pearlescent cool glow from the sky. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 有没有硬光束 · 影子是否柔和 · 光是不是弥散
06🌈

Color · 克制配色

测: Image 2 能否做"muted 调性" · 不饱和 · 不亮金 · 这是上版失败的那条
A landscape with one small figure, using a restrained muted color palette only: mossy green, weathered stone grey, earth ochre, silvered violet, deep indigo. NO saturated colors, NO bright gold, NO neon, NO high-contrast metallics. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 饱和度控住没 · 有没有偷偷加亮金 · 色调是否克制
07📐

Composition · 构图比例

测: Image 2 能否按"1/8 人 + 3/4 天空"硬规定构图 · 不放大主体
A landscape where the figure and ground occupy ONLY the lower 1/4 of the frame, vast sky dominates the upper 3/4, the figure is very small (about 1/8 of frame), asymmetric placement at lower-left third. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 人是不是真的小 · 天空占比够大不 · 位置对不对
08🌙

Mood · 情绪词

测: Image 2 对**文学 / 哲学情绪词**的理解力(Sehnsucht = 德语"对无限的渴望")
A landscape evoking Sehnsucht — 19th-century German romantic longing for the infinite, contemplative, melancholic awe, poetic solitude, a sense of yearning for the beyond. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 这种抽象情绪模型能表现吗 · 画面是否"有诗意"vs"只是好看"
09🌌

Subject Mix · 宇宙天空

测: Image 2 能否把"宇宙 / 星云 / 行星"塞进"绘画风"而不滑向 CGI 科幻
A landscape with an impossibly vast cosmic vista filling the sky — muted violet nebula, pale dusty rose, pinprick stars, a single silvered ringed planet low on the horizon, rendered with the luminous restraint of a 19th-century romantic oil painting, not a sci-fi render. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 宇宙是"画出来的"还是"渲染出来的" · 两个审美冲突能否融合
10✍️

Text · 签名式小字

测: 引号语法 + 签名场景("像画家签在画上")· 小字渲染精度
A simple painting of rocks and sky. At the lower-right corner, a faint painted signature-style inscription reads exactly: "OPERA COSMICA · MMXXVI", rendered as if handwritten by the painter into the rock's shadow, darkly integrated, subtle and almost hidden. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 引号内容一字不差吗 · 罗马数字对不对 · 风格是签名感还是海报字
11🚫

Negative · 禁用约束

测: Image 2 对"NOT add X"的执行力 · 禁用原子能否独立生效
A landscape with one small figure. Strictly DO NOT include: baroque architecture, golden ornament, columns, candelabras, ornate decoration, high-saturation colors, dramatic lighting, hyperreal photographic detail, modern objects, multiple figures, crowds, any text. aspect ratio 16:9.
👁️ 看: 禁用项有没有偷偷出现 · Image 2 能否"守规矩"
